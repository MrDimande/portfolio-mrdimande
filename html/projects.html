    Resource Diagnosis in the Territorial Planning and Management Process: A Case Study of Zimpeto Neighbourhood

    This study explores how resource assessment can drive smarter urban planning in rapidly growing areas. Focusing on Zimpeto, Maputo, it uncovers the challenges of unplanned expansion, infrastructure gaps, and environmental risks. Through data-driven analysis and field research, the study proposes practical strategies for sustainable and inclusive urban development. With insights on land use, infrastructure, and community participation, it offers a roadmap for better territorial management.
